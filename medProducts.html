Advantages

Hygiene / Ventilation
Robo Cast provides excellent ventilation, thus preventing itching, foul smells or skin diseases due to moisture inside the cast. Also, if the stockinet cotton bandage made of water repellent material is used, you can take a shower or swim in the pool.

(In such cases, it should be dried properly using a hairdryer set to cool air, and consultation with a doctor is required in advance.)

Prevention of dripping

Robo Cast Product

forming phenomenon occurred due to the dripping down of resin.

RoboCast contains substances to minimize resin shedding during long-term storage.

plaster bandage

Robocast polyester products have superior X-ray transparency compared to glass fiber products, making it easier to diagnose patients and observe their recovery status, especially the microfracture area.
small amount of dust

polyester

glass fiber

Robocast polyester products generate a small amount of dust when removed after use compared to glass fiber products, and unlike glass fiber dust, they do not scatter into the air or go down.
(In fact, the glass fiber dust (particles) that are harmful to the human body are so small that they are not filtered out by the filters of common vacuum aspirators used in hospitals.)
environmental friendliness
Robocast polyester products are woven from 100% polyester and can be incinerated at a designated incinerator after use, preventing soil pollution from disposing of waste glass fiber.
