Robo TM Casting Tape

Advantages

Curing speed control
The curing speed can be controlled by the temperature and amount of water used.
- When you feel that hardening is fast (summer): Soak the product in water for 3 to 5 seconds, then take it out, shake off the water, and apply.
- When you feel that hardening is slow (winter): Soak the product in water and knead it 3 to 5 times, then take it out of the water and squeeze it again 2 to 3 times to remove the water before performing the treatment.
S & F patented substances
RoboCast contains S&F's patented substances, so it has less stickiness during treatment and has excellent molding properties.
less pressure
Robocast polyester products have good formability due to their excellent elasticity, and because they have weak rebound and recovery force after stretching, there is a low possibility of pressure on the affected area.
Flexibility
Robocast has excellent elasticity in length, width, and diagonal directions, making it advantageous for molding curved areas of the body such as elbows and heels.
Hygiene / Ventilation
Robo Cast provides excellent ventilation, thus preventing itching, foul smells or skin diseases due to moisture inside the cast. Also, if the stockinet cotton bandage made of water repellent material is used, you can take a shower or swim in the pool.

(In such cases, it should be dried properly using a hairdryer set to cool air, and consultation with a doctor is required in advance.)

Environmental friendliness
Robocast polyester products are woven from 100% polyester and can be incinerated at a designated incinerator after use, preventing soil pollution from disposing of waste glass fiber.
